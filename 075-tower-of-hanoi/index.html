🗼 Tower of Hanoi

Moves: 0  |  Optimal: 7
How to play: Move all disks from the left peg to the right peg. You can only move one disk at a time, and a larger disk cannot be placed on top of a smaller one. Click a peg to pick up the top disk, then click another peg to place it. Try to solve it in the minimum number of moves!
